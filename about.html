Our Story: A Pint of Passion, A Dash of Community

Nestled in the heart of Woodinville, Rooneys is more than just a gastropub — it’s a community hub where locals and travelers alike gather for unforgettable meals and warm memories. Inspired by traditional Irish pubs and infused with the innovative culinary spirit of the Pacific Northwest, Rooneys offers a menu and ambiance unlike any other.

From hearty Guinness stew to seasonal cocktails crafted with local ingredients, we blend old-world comfort with new-world creativity. Our interior is designed to evoke both rustic charm and modern sophistication, with dim lighting, warm woods, and inviting leather accents. Every detail is curated to make you feel right at home, yet transported to a lively Irish cottage.

Whether you're here for brunch with friends, a pint after work, or a romantic dinner, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere and exceptional service waiting. We believe in good food, good drink, and even better company. Join us at Rooneys and discover a place where tradition meets taste, and every visit feels like coming home.
