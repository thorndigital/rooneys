Our Story, Our Spirit

At Rooneys, we blend the warmth of traditional Irish hospitality with the vibrant culinary scene of Woodinville. Our gastropub offers a cozy atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy hearty food, a wide selection of craft beers, and premium spirits.

Inspired by the rich heritage of Ireland, every dish is crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring an authentic and unforgettable dining experience. Come for the food, stay for the craic!
